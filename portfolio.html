  • each gallery image evokes a drop-down modal (like this one!), useful for additional info via embedded text or video.

Bravoflix

A comprehensive, highly performant video content management system built on Pylons (a Python framework); Designed in collaboration with Simple Station, it was the first subscription video service of its kind in the U.S. to focus exclusively on the performing arts. The site's functionality is comparable to YouTube at a fraction of the cost for existing SaaS platforms or self-hosted video solutions.

  • user profiles and roles (e.g., subscribers vs. editors)
  • monthly subscription billing and payment gateway integration
  • administrative dashboard
  • calendarized content publication
  • multi-bitrate encoding of multiple video formats
  • storage of all videos in Amazon S3; uncensored global delivery of rich media via CloudFront CDN.
  • SEO optimization, and searchable categories and tags for all video content
